About SI Studio

Saint Ignatious studio is a professional studio based in Johanessburg. We provide everything from music production, video and photo shooting at an affordable price. Apart from being a record studio we also offer high quality training on sound engineering and keyboard technics.

We have a very talented and experienced team that are highly skilled in the entertaiment industry. Our goal is to offer services at an affordable price while still maintaining our high standard.
